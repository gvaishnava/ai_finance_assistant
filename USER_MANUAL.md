# AI Finance Assistant - User Manual

Welcome to the **AI Finance Assistant**, a multi-agent system designed to help you learn about investing, analyze your portfolio, and plan your financial future using real-time market data and an extensive financial knowledge base.

---

## 🚀 Navigation Overview

The application is divided into **7 specialized tabs**, each powered by a dedicated AI agent:

1.  **💬 Chat**: General financial education and Q&A.
2.  **📊 Market**: Real-time stock quotes and AI-driven market perspectives.
3.  **📰 News**: Live financial news search and impact analysis.
4.  **💼 Portfolio**: Educational analysis of your stock holdings.
5.  **🎯 Goals**: Financial goal setting, progress tracking, and milestone planning.
6.  **📑 Tax**: Guidance on Indian taxation rules for investors.
7.  **👤 Profile**: Personalization settings for AI explanations.

---

## 💬 1. Chat Interface
*The entry point for general financial learning.*

-   **Ask Anything**: Type questions like "What is an Index Fund?" or "How does compound interest work?".
-   **Suggestions**: Click on suggested topic chips (e.g., "Mutual fund basics") to start a conversation quickly.
-   **Citations**: The AI provides sources from the Zerodha stock market course for its educational responses.
-   **Visualizations**: Some answers include charts (e.g., compounding visualizers) to help explain concepts.

---

## 📊 2. Market Dashboard
*Monitor the markets and get AI perspectives on specific stocks.*

-   **Major Indices**: View real-time tracking of **Nifty 50**, **Sensex**, and **Nifty Bank**.
-   **Stock Search**: Enter any symbol (use `.NS` for NSE or `.BO` for BSE, e.g., `RELIANCE.NS`) to get live pricing and sector info.
-   **⚡ Get AI Perspective**: After searching a stock, click this button to receive a detailed analysis of the stock's performance and market standing.
-   **Past Insights**: Access your previous stock analyses from the gallery at the bottom.

---

## 📰 3. News Dashboard
*Stay updated with real-time intelligence.*

-   **Fetch News**: Search for keywords like "Tata Motors", "Inflation", or "US Tech" to pull the latest headlines.
-   **⚡ AI Impact Analysis**: Click this on any news article. The **News Agent** will synthesize the article and explain its potential impact on the markets.
-   **Analysis History**: Use the sidebar to quickly switch between different article analyses.

---

## 💼 4. Portfolio Analysis
*Understand the health and diversification of your investments.*

-   **Add Holdings**: Input your stock symbols, quantity, and average purchase price.
-   **Analyze Portfolio**: Click to trigger the **Portfolio Agent**. It will review your allocation, sector concentration, and provide educational tips on diversification.
-   **Visualizations**: View your portfolio breakdown in interactive Pie or Bar charts.

---

## 🎯 5. Financial Goal Planner
*Plan your path to financial freedom.*

-   **＋ Add New Goal**: Set goals for Retirement, Education, Home Purchase, etc.
-   **Progress Tracking**: The app uses your "Target Date" and "Already Saved" amount to calculate a progress percentage and show a visual progress bar.
-   **Analyze**: Get a detailed AI-generated roadmap for reaching a specific goal.
-   **📂 History**: Click this on any goal card to **filter the analysis panel**. It will show only the history and charts relevant to *that specific goal*.

---

## 📑 6. Tax Assistant
*Navigate Indian tax laws with ease.*

-   **Specialized Knowledge**: Ask about Capital Gains (LTCG/STCG), Section 80C exemptions, or how dividends are taxed.
-   **Quick Topics**: Use the sidebar for one-click questions on common tax hurdles.
-   **Important**: Always note the disclaimer; the agent provides education, but you should consult a CA for official filing.

---

## 👤 7. User Profile
*Tailor the AI's personality to your needs.*

-   **Knowledge Level**: Choose **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, or **Advanced**. The AI will simplify or deepen its technical language accordingly.
-   **Risk Tolerance**: Set your profile to **Conservative**, **Moderate**, or **Aggressive**. This influences the diversification advice and goal planning strategies the AI suggests.
-   **Save Profile**: Your settings are saved across tabs to ensure a consistent experience.

---

## 🛠️ Tips for the Best Experience

1.  **Use Correct Symbols**: For Indian stocks, always append the exchange code: `RELIANCE.NS` (NSE) or `RELIANCE.BO` (BSE).
2.  **Context is Key**: Each tab has its own localized history to keep your research organized.
3.  **Real-time Refresh**: Market indices refresh every 60 seconds automatically.
4.  **Frozen Terminal?**: If the app seems stuck, click into the terminal window and press **Enter** (to exit Windows QuickEdit mode).
